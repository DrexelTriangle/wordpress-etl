    i. Enter first name 
    ii. Enter last name
    iii. Enter email

        a. Confirm
 3. This author is actually multiple authors.
    i. New Author, existing authors
    i. Enter author numbers, separated by dashes
 4. Skip for now.
 5. Exit 
 6. Back
 7. Invalid character
